Personal Details:

Full Name: Worker's legal full name.
Contact Information: Phone number, email address.
Residential Address: Street address, city, province, postal code (likely linked to the WorkerProfile entity fields: phone_number, address, city, province, postal_code, gps_coordinates, formatted_address).
Date of Birth: For age verification and legal employment requirements.
Work Eligibility:

Right to Work Status: Confirmation of legal eligibility to work in the relevant jurisdiction (e.g., Canadian citizen, permanent resident, valid work permit holder).
Social Insurance Number (SIN) / Tax ID: Collected post-hire, but acknowledgement of requirement during application.
Background Check Consent: Agreement for the company to conduct necessary background checks.
Role Interests:

Preferred Job Roles: Selection from a predefined list of roles (e.g., Housekeeper, Server, Cook, General Labor, as defined in TodoItem.assigned_workers.worker_role). An "Other" option with a free-text field for roles not listed.
Availability:

General Availability: Days of the week and preferred time blocks (e.g., mornings, afternoons, evenings) the worker is generally available.
Specific Dates/Periods of Unavailability: Any known periods the worker cannot work.
Experience:

Relevant Work History: Previous job titles, company names, dates of employment, and a brief description of duties for each role.
Years of Experience: Overall or per role (potentially stored in WorkerSkill.years_of_experience).
Skills:

Skill Set: List of specific skills relevant to the job roles (e.g., equipment operation, software proficiency, language fluency, safety certifications). These would directly map to the WorkerSkill entity (skill_name, skill_category, proficiency_level).
Certifications/Licenses: Any relevant certifications or licenses, including expiry dates (e.g., WorkerSkill.certification_number, WorkerSkill.expiry_date).
Self-Certification: Workers declare their skills, which may require later admin verification (WorkerSkill.self_certified, WorkerSkill.admin_verified).
Shift Preferences:

Shift Type Preference: Day shifts, night shifts, weekdays, weekends.
Desired Shift Length: E.g., 4-hour, 8-hour.
Travel Willingness: How far the worker is willing to travel for shifts.
Emergency Contacts:

Name: Full name of emergency contact.
Relationship: Relationship to the worker.
Phone Number: Contact number for emergencies (stored in WorkerProfile.emergency_contact_name, WorkerProfile.emergency_contact_phone).
Documentation Uploads:

Resume/CV: Upload of the worker's resume.
Work Permits/Visas: If applicable, proof of right to work.
Certificates/Licenses: Scanned copies of relevant qualifications.
Void Cheque/Banking Information: Explicitly stated that this is collected post-hire, but the form confirms understanding of this requirement (eventual WorkerProfile.banking_details).
(All document URLs would be stored in WorkerProfile.document_urls).
Acknowledgments and Consents:

TITO Accuracy: Acknowledgment of the Time-In/Time-Out (TITO) system and the importance of accurate clocking in/out, including GPS validation (WorkerProfile.reminder_preference and general understanding of TITO system parameters like ShiftRequest.geofence_radius_meters).
Site Rules: Agreement to adhere to client site-specific rules and regulations.
Worker Agreement: Consent to the terms and conditions of the general worker agreement (likely refers to WorkerProfile.subcontractor_agreement_signed, WorkerProfile.agreement_signed_date, WorkerProfile.subcontractor_agreement_uri).
Privacy Consents: Agreement to the company's privacy policy, outlining how personal data is collected, used, and protected, in compliance with GDPR and PIPEDA.
Marketing/Communication Consent: Optional consent for receiving promotional communications.
Electronic Signature:

A secure electronic signature mechanism (WorkerDocumentStatus.signature_data) to formally acknowledge and agree to all terms, conditions, and information provided in the application. This ensures legal enforceability of the worker agreement.
This detailed form ensures that all critical worker data is captured upfront, streamlining the onboarding process and providing HR with a comprehensive profile for each applicant. Some fields, like banking details, are explicitly deferred to the post-hire stage to maintain privacy during the initial application phase.
